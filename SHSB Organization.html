Project: Hope

Content

Documentary/
Educational Videos

The reason why we chose documentary/educational videos is that they have been widely popular these days, especially with viewers. Due to the uproar trend of getting an adequate amount of knowledge with additional entertainment. We believe that using this category will give more awareness to people who are uninformed about the circumstances like Sitio Bebe. People are filled with curiosity if you entice them with a good thumbnail and title. They are hungry for knowledge, so using documentaries or videos that are educational can lead to viewers becoming more aware of the situation. Educational/Documentary provide a plethora of details, which can be used as a way to showcase the health crisis in Sitio Bebe.

Audience

Target Audience

  • Sponsors/Philanthropists
  • Teens and Youth
  • Adults


Needs

  • The audience(Sponsors/Philanthropist) needs adequate information about the project goals.
  • The audience (teens and youths) needs to be informed about the current situation in Sitio Bebe. They must be engaged and influenced by assisting and contributing to the fundraiser's cause.
  • The audience (adults) needs to be aware of how crises such as those in Sitio Bebe affect the civilians who live there. They need to be better informed about things like health disasters so that they can help and contribute to preventing them from occurring.

About our Fundraiser

Project: Hope is a non-government fundraising event, the main objective is to have a concert for Sitio Bebe. The organization has set out the goal of helping the community to lessen the health crisis. The group decided to raise funds and create awareness through having a concert.


Project: Hope is a way to give a light of hope to those who needed help and are in dire need of assistance. This is a fundraising event organized by the SHSB Organization (Twitter Chatters).
